Submission Guidelines

Please review these guidelines carefully before submitting a record:

  • Your video must be uncut and have clicks completely audible the whole way through. This ensures authenticity and helps staff verify your run.
  • Your completion video must be uploaded to YouTube or another recognized video-based platform. Direct social media links (TikTok, Instagram) are not permitted.
  • You must have a visible cheat indicator if your mod menu has one. This helps verify that no disallowed modifications were used.
  • The LDM/Bugfix copy of a level has to, without a doubt, not make a significant difference in the level. Minor graphical changes are fine, but any change that simplifies gameplay is forbidden.
  • Your record can be valid if it ends midway through the endscreen, ONLY if it's impossible to die in the endscreen. If the last possible point of death has passed, the record is acceptable.
  • If your record is in the Top 15, you must submit raw footage, with split audio tracks in a downloadable format (e.g., Google Drive/Dropbox). This is a mandatory measure for the hardest levels on the list.
  • Your record cannot contain any disallowed mods or cheats; Click Between Frames (CBF) is allowed, but physics bypass is not.
  • During your completion, you cannot use skips that make the level significantly easier. This includes major route deviations not intended by the creator.

For any questions regarding a submission, please contact the staff via Discord.
