The Former Hardest Levels List (FHLL) tracks and ranks the former hardest levels in Geometry Dash (including the current hardest).
Levels are ranked according to their historical difficulty and not necessarily their current status on lists like the Demonlist or ILL.

Submission Guidelines

Please ensure your record complies with the following guidelines:

  • Your videos must be uploaded at full speed, clearly displaying all clicks and the entire completion.
  • **Raw footage is required for all levels ranked in the Top 15.** Raw footage means the completion recorded locally before any editing, ideally with sound and showing proof of the start of the attempt.
  • Completion videos must be uploaded to YouTube or another recognized video sharing platform (Vimeo, BiliBili). Social media (TikTok, Instagram) links are not permitted.
  • Videos may not be deleted after acceptance. If you delete the video, the record will be removed.
  • Do not use illegal or hacked versions of the game. Verification status may be checked by list staff.
  • Display the level's ID, star count, and demon difficulty (if applicable) clearly at the beginning or end of the video.

For any questions, please contact the staff via Discord.
